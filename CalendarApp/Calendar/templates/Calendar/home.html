Welcome to CalendarCalc!

What does CalendarCalc do?

CalendarCalc creates a summary of how much time a user has spent or will spend per activity on his/her current week. After the summary is created, it can be saved and accessed at any time for future reference!

How CalendarCalc does it?

CalendarCalc gets the week schedules of our users through Google Calendar. At the moment of creating a new summary, just let us know from which Google account we will be getting your schedule! To create a new summary click on the New button

Why CalendarCalc does it?

CalendarCalc aims to be an useful tool for Google Calendar users, helping them improve their time-management habits if needed or inform them that they are doing a great work managing their weekly schedules!
