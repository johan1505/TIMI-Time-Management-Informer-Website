Welcome, My name is TIMI!

What does TIMI do?

TIMI creates a summary of how much time a user has spent or will spend per event on his/her current week. After the summary is created, it can be saved and accessed at any time for future reference!

How does TIMI do it?

TIMI collects data of users' week schedules through Google Calendar. At the moment of creating a new summary, just let TIM know from which Google account he will be getting your schedule! To create a new summary click on the New button

Why does TIMI do it?

TIMI aims to be a useful tool for Google Calendar users, informing them about their time-management habits and provide them a nice data visualization doughnut chart of the data gathered
