Welcome to CalendarCalc!

What does CalendarCalc do?

CalendarCalc creates a Summary of how much time a user have spent or will spend per activity on the the current week. After the summary is created, it can be saved and accessed at any time for future reference!

How CalendarCalc does it?

CalendarCalc gets the week schedule of the user through Google Calendar. At the moment of creating a new summary, just let us know from which Google account we will be getting the schedule!

Why CalendarCalc does it?

CalendarCalc aims to be a useful tool for Google Calendar users. Its main goal is to help users improve their time-management habits if needed or inform them that they are doing a great work managing their weekly schedule!
